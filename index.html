About me

I am a research engineer in the Cyber Security Research Group at the Qatar Computing Research Institute. I received my BSc in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University.

My research interests include backdoor attacks and defenses in deep learning, robust machine learning, security of LLMs, federated learning, and the ethics of AI.
